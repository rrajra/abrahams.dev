Left Right Center

Description

My inspiration for this game was that I wanted to make a game that I could play with family and friends anywhere, and not have to worry about bringing the physical copy of the game. As a result, I learned multiplayer networking and started work on this game. For the networking component, it uses Mirror and the Unity Engine.
